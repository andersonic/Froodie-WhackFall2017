This site is meant to allow people to drop off edible but unwanted food so that those who are not fortunate enough to be able to afford food can eat with dignity. Do not drop off and log food that is unfit for human consumption. Additionally, those who chose to eat food found through this site do so at their own risk. Exercise common sense in choosing what to eat and in where you go to retrieve it.
